Clean Shores, Bright Future:

The Advantages of Keeping Our Beaches Oil-Free

Securing our beaches from oil spills protects marine life, ensures safe recreational areas, boosts tourism, and preserves the natural beauty of our coastline.

Ecosystem

Saving marine environments from oil spills protects diverse ecosystems and the species within them.

Biodiversity

Prevention preserves marine biodiversity, maintaining the balance of aquatic life.

Economy

Healthy oceans support industries like fisheries and tourism, ensuring economic resilience.

Health

Avoiding contamination safeguards seafood safety and reduces health risks.

Climate

Healthy oceans mitigate climate change impacts by absorbing carbon dioxide.

Culture

Preservation honors coastal traditions and cultural heritage tied to the sea.

Sri Lanka Marine Environment
Protection Authority

The Marine Environment Protection Authority abbreviated MEPA is the apex body established by the Government of Sri Lanka, under the provisions of Marine Pollution Prevention Act No.59 of 1981, which was later repealed by Marine Pollution Prevention Act No.35 of 2008.
