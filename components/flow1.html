Inspection
Inspection
Under oil spill contingency plan regulation No.01 of 2012, oil handling agencies must conduct regular inspections to assess spill response capabilities, equipment effectiveness, and personnel training adequacy. They also update response plans, ensure resource sufficiency, maintain documentation, and facilitate communication with relevant agencies.  ​
